FAQs

How do I sign up?

Signing up is easy! Just click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, enter your details, and you’ll be ready to start chatting in no time.

Is my data safe with Linkify?

Yes! Linkify uses end-to-end encryption to ensure that your messages are always private and secure.

What features does Linkify offer?

Linkify offers features such as one-click sign-up, private messaging, group chats, and multimedia sharing.

How do I reset my password?

If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset it.

Is Linkify free to use?

Yes, Linkify is completely free to use with optional premium features available for enhanced functionality.
